Objectives

Providing engaging opportunities for young people to understand the current situation of equal opportunities in Hong Kong and raising public awareness of the four anti-discrimination ordinances to eliminate the attitude of discrimination through participating in debate competitions.

Eligibility

All secondary schools or international schools registered with the Education Bureau are eligible to participate in the competition. Each participating school can send one team to take part in the competition, and participants of each team must be students at that school during the competition.



Each participating school must:

  • Appoint at least one qualified mentor (such as someone with experience in debate, general studies, or language teaching) to serve as an adjudicator for the first round, second round, and up to the semifinals.

  • Provide a competition venue for at least one day, which must include rooms for the competitions/school auditorium, a judges’ lounge, and two preparation rooms (for both the affirmative and negative teams).

  • Once the mentors appointed by the participating schools are confirmed by the Organizing Committee (hereafter the Organizer) and arranged to serve as adjudicators for the competition on the designated day, they must attend punctually. If they are unable to attend due to reasons, they must notify the Organizer three days before the competition and provide a substitute mentor to fulfill the adjudicating role.

  • Each school is limited to sending only one team to participate.

  • Each team should consist of 4 to 10 members, 4 of whom as formal representatives of the school for the competition and the remaining 1 to 6 members as standbys.

  • Each team must appoint at least one representative to attend a briefing session scheduled for January 25, 2025 (tentative).



General Rules

  1. The competition will be conducted in Cantonese and on a knockout basis. Participation is limited to 32 teams. Winning teams of each round of the competition are qualified for the next round of the competition. If the number of applications exceeds the participation quota, the participating schools will be selected through a draw.

  2. The debate motions and competition dates for each match will be decided by the Organizer, and participants may not raise objections. The assignment of teams to affirmative and opposite sides will be determined by a draw conducted by the Organizer.

  1. Arrangements for Late Arrival and Absence: If any team is late for 15 minutes or more on the day of the competition, the team will be disqualified, and their opponent will automatically advance to the next round.

  2. Authority of the Organizer: The Organizer reserves the right and authority to invite anyone to serve as adjudicators, advisors, and chairpersons in the competition.

First Runner-up

Book vouchers valued at HKD 4,000 and a trophy.

Third Place

Book vouchers valued at HKD 2,000 and a trophy.



Best Debater of the Grand Final

Book vouchers valued at HKD 800 and a trophy.

Best Debater of other rounds

Book vouchers valued at HKD 300 and a certificate.






*The aforementioned awards will be presented on the day of the awards ceremony, following the conclusion of the grand final.
